Events and Webinars

At Girl Tech, we believe that knowledge grows stronger when it’s shared. Our events and seminars are designed to spark ideas, build confidence, and create meaningful connections across the tech world.

From live coding workshops and panel discussions to fireside chats with industry leaders, we host a wide range of virtual and in-person events tailored to our community.

What to expect

  • Skill-building workshops: Learn everything from Python basics to UX design principles led by women in tech.
  • Networking sessions: Connect with like-minded members in your region or industry.
  • Panel discussions: Hear from trailblazers about breaking barriers, career pivots, and tech trends.
  • Mentorship circles: Join small-group sessions to get guidance and share experiences.

Members-only access

To keep our events meaningful and interactive, many sessions are exclusive to registered Girl Tech members.
